About Corrie

I wrote my first lines of code in middle school, and programmed a simple hangman game. I remember being fascinated that some words on my screen could be understood by my computer and I could create things using just the right combination of words. The unfortunate thing was that I was encouraged to study other subjects in school because computer engineering was not considered traditional female work, which my well-meaning parents had implied at that point.

Fast forward a couple of decades, and I've returned to the fascination that I once discovered as a young girl. As a student at Thinkful, I've begun my journey to becoming a software developer and hope to encourage other atypical students too!

If you're looking to hire a new developer, please reach out! I am so excited to work with other developers to build great software.
